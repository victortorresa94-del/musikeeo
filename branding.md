## üåà Color Palette

### Primary
- **Black (Background):** `#0A0A0A` (Deep, pure black)
- **Charcoal (Surface):** `#171717` (Slightly lighter, for cards/sections)
- **Yellow (Accent/Brand):** `#FFD84D` (Electric but warm yellow)

### Secondary
- **Text White:** `#FFFFFF` (Primary text)
- **Text Grey:** `#B0B0B0` (Secondary text, descriptions)
- **Border/Divider:** `#1E1E1E` or `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)`

### Functional
- **Success:** `#22C55E`
- **Error:** `#EF4444`
- **Warning:** `#F59E0B`
